The Finance module is the central ledger for SCA Academia. It records every rupee that flows in and out of the academy — from student fee collections to staff payouts to operational expenses — and provides the owner with dashboards, history filters, and printable reports. All financial activity originates in one of two places: the [fee collection](/modules/fee-collection) flow or a manually recorded transaction.

## Transaction types

Every financial event is stored as a `Transaction` document with a `type` field that determines how it is reported and aggregated.

| Type | Direction | Description |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| `INCOME` | In | Revenue collected — tuition fees, misc student payments (trip, lab, event) |
| `EXPENSE` | Out | Cash paid out — rent, utilities, teacher payouts, salaries |
| `CREDIT` | Internal | Teacher share allocated from a fee payment to a teacher's pending balance |
| `LIABILITY` | Internal | Manual credit entry recording an amount the academy owes |
| `REFUND` | Out | Reversal of a previously collected payment |

### Transaction categories

Categories provide a second level of classification within each type. Common categories include **Tuition**, **Teacher Payout**, **Teacher Advance**, **Teacher Salary**, **Rent**, **Utilities**, **Salaries**, **Electricity Bill**, **Stationery**, **Equipment/Asset**, and **Misc**.

## Transaction status lifecycle

Every transaction moves through a defined set of statuses that reflect whether the cash has been physically reconciled.

```text
FLOATING  →  VERIFIED  →  (REFUNDED)
```

- **FLOATING** — cash is in the collector's hands but has not yet been counted and confirmed. All fee income enters as `FLOATING`.
- **VERIFIED** — cash has been reconciled during daily closing. Expense transactions are recorded directly as `VERIFIED` because they represent cash already spent.
- **REFUNDED** — a previously collected amount has been reversed.

Only `INCOME` transactions go through the `FLOATING → VERIFIED` transition. `EXPENSE` and `CREDIT` transactions are written as `VERIFIED` immediately.

## Daily closing workflow

Daily closing is how staff reconcile floating cash at the end of each working day. It converts all `FLOATING` income transactions into `VERIFIED` and updates teacher balances accordingly.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1. Review floating transactions">
    Before closing, the staff member checks all `FLOATING` income transactions assigned to their account. These represent cash they have physically collected but not yet handed over or deposited. The total is visible in the finance dashboard as **Floating Cash**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2. Count and verify physical cash">
    The collector counts their on-hand cash and confirms it matches the sum of their floating transactions. Any discrepancy must be investigated before proceeding.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="3. Submit the close-day request">
    The staff member clicks the **Close Day** action, which sends a `POST /api/finance/close-day` request. The backend finds all `FLOATING` income transactions for that user, sums them, and creates a `DailyClosing` record.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="4. Transactions move to VERIFIED">
    All matching `FLOATING` transactions are bulk-updated to `status: "VERIFIED"` and linked to the `DailyClosing` document by its ID. The closing record captures the total amount, transaction count, date, and closing staff member.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="5. Teacher balances update">
    For any transaction that carries a `splitDetails.teacherId`, the teacher's `balance.floating` amount is moved to `balance.verified`. This makes the teacher's earnings available for payout requests. See [Payroll](/modules/payroll) for what happens next.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Recording expenses

Staff and owners can log manual expenses directly from the Finance page. An expense requires:

- **Category** — selected from the predefined list (Rent, Utilities, Salaries, etc.)
- **Amount** — must be greater than zero
- **Description** — a brief note about the expense
- **Date** — defaults to today; can be adjusted for past entries

Expense transactions are created with `type: "EXPENSE"` and `status: "VERIFIED"` immediately, because they represent cash that has already left the academy. They appear in the unified finance history and reduce the net profit figure on the dashboard.

## Finance overview page

The Finance page is organized into tabs. The overview tab surfaces:

- **Monthly income** — sum of all `INCOME` transactions in the current calendar month (gross, before refunds)
- **Monthly expenses** — sum of all `EXPENSE` transactions this month
- **Net profit** — monthly income minus monthly expenses
- **Floating cash** — total unverified `INCOME` still pending daily closing
- **Pending fees** — all-time difference between total expected fees and total collected fees
- **Teacher liabilities** — sum of all active teachers' floating + verified + pending balances

A searchable transaction history table below the stats cards shows all transactions merged with expense records and teacher payment vouchers, sorted newest first.

## Finance records (FinanceRecord model)

In addition to the main `Transaction` collection, the system maintains a `FinanceRecord` collection for historical fee payment records. Each `FinanceRecord` stores:

- `receiptId` — unique receipt identifier
- `studentId` and `studentName` — the paying student
- `studentClass` — class at time of payment
- `totalFee`, `paidAmount`, `balance` — fee amounts
- `status` — `paid`, `partial`, or `pending`
- `paymentMethod` — cash, bank-transfer, cheque, or online
- `month` and `year` — period the payment covers

Finance records can be filtered by `status`, `month`, and `year` through the `GET /api/finance` endpoint.

## Transaction history filters

The finance history endpoint (`GET /api/finance/history`) accepts the following query parameters:

| Parameter | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| `type` | Filter by transaction type (`INCOME`, `EXPENSE`, `ALL`) |
| `startDate` | ISO date string — lower bound for the transaction date |
| `endDate` | ISO date string — upper bound for the transaction date |
| `page` | Page number for pagination (default: `1`) |
| `limit` | Results per page (default: `50`) |

Results include merged data from `Transaction`, `Expense`, and `TeacherPayment` collections, all sorted by date descending.

## Financial reports

The owner can generate a printable financial report for a defined period via `GET /api/finance/generate-report`. Supported period values are `today`, `week`, `month`, and `custom` (requires `startDate` and `endDate`). The report includes revenue by category, expenses by category, total fees collected, net profit, and a generation timestamp.
